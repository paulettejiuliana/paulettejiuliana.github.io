AboutMe

Hey I am Paulette! I am a graphic design student, currently in my third year of exploration. I am captivated by illustration and analog processes, where the essence of art resides in the journey itself. My passion spills into watercolors and the pages of my sketchbooks, where I interwine drawing and writing, crafting an eternal dance of ideas and emotions. Welcome to some of my work and enojoy having a look!
